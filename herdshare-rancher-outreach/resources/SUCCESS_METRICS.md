# HerdShare — Success Metrics & Performance Dashboard

## Purpose

This document defines the key performance indicators (KPIs) for measuring the effectiveness of HerdShare's rancher lead generation and outreach system.

**Goal:** Track, measure, and optimize every stage of the rancher acquisition funnel from lead sourcing to long-term retention.

---

## Metrics Framework

### The Rancher Acquisition Funnel

```
Lead Sourcing (Top of Funnel)
    ↓
Lead Qualification (Interest)
    ↓
Outreach & Engagement (Conversations)
    ↓
Onboarding (First Delivery)
    ↓
Retention (Ongoing Partnership)
    ↓
Referrals (Multiplication)
```

**Track metrics at every stage.**

---

## Core Metrics Dashboard

### Weekly Tracking (Primary KPIs)

| Metric | Definition | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|------------|--------|----------------|
| **Leads Sourced** | New rancher leads added to CRM | 30-50 | Pipeline health |

| **Referrals Received** | Leads from existing rancher partners | 1-3 | Referral flywheel activation |

## Cost & Economics Metrics

### Unit Economics

| Metric | Definition | Target | Calculation |
|--------|------------|--------|-------------|
| **Cost per Lead** | Total spend / Leads sourced | $15-30 | (Staff time + tools + ads) / Leads |
| **Cost per Conversation** | Total spend / Conversations held | $50-75 | Total spend / Conversations |
| **Cost per Onboarded Rancher** | Total spend / Ranchers onboarded | $400-700 | Total spend / Onboarded |
| **Payback Period** | Time to recover acquisition cost | <12 months | Based on margin per rancher |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | Lifetime value : Customer acquisition cost | 5:1+ | Rancher lifetime margin / CAC |

## Dashboard Reporting Cadence

### Weekly Dashboard (Every Monday)

**Purpose:** Tactical activity review and week planning.

**Metrics to Review:**
- Leads sourced last week
- Calls made and conversations held
- Meetings scheduled
- Follow-ups completed
- Next week's priorities

**Format:** 15-minute team standup or email summary.

---

### Monthly Dashboard (First Week of Month)

**Purpose:** Strategic performance review and optimization.

**Metrics to Review:**
- Lead generation (volume, sources, scores)
- Outreach effectiveness (contact rate, conversion rates)
- Onboarding (ranchers onboarded, time to onboard)
- Costs (cost per lead, cost per onboard)
- Referrals (referrals received, referral conversion)
- Retention (active partnerships, churn)

**Format:** 60-minute team meeting with leadership.

---

### Quarterly Dashboard (End of Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)

**Purpose:** Long-term trend analysis and strategic planning.

**Metrics to Review:**
- Quarterly goals vs. actuals
- Regional performance ranking
- Channel ROI analysis
- Retention and LTV trends
- Referral flywheel momentum
- Year-over-year growth (if applicable)

**Format:** Half-day strategic review with leadership + team.

## Red Flags & Alerts

### When to Worry (Metrics Below Target)

| Red Flag | Threshold | Action |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| **Lead volume declining** | <20 leads/week | Reactivate sourcing channels, expand regions |
| **Contact rate dropping** | <50% | Review phone numbers quality, call timing |
| **Conversation rate dropping** | <40% | Revisit phone scripts, improve opening |
| **Interest rate dropping** | <30% | Refine value proposition, address objections better |
| **Onboarding rate stalling** | <1/month | Review onboarding bottlenecks, increase in-person visits |
| **Cost per onboard rising** | >$1,000 | Reduce costs or improve conversion rates |
| **Churn increasing** | >10%/year | Survey churned ranchers, fix service issues |
| **Referrals declining** | <1/month | Ask more consistently, improve partner satisfaction |
